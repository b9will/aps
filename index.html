In Good Company

APS is what happens when Canada's top commercial producers stop competing and start collaborating. Built for US, UK, and international production companies, we transform the economic advantages of shooting in Canada into creative leverage — more production value, with fewer constraints.

Most service companies are bolt-ons: built around a single producer, or an existing director roster, with international servicing as an afterthought. APS is different by design. We are a collective of over ten specialist line producers, each with distinct skillsets and category expertise — ensuring availability, depth of experience, and the right fit for every project.

Our speciality is translating currency advantages, world-class infrastructure, and trusted relationships into bigger scope, smarter spend, and seamless execution. So if you're considering Canada, we're the first call.
